**Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.

Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps.

Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks.

Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails or messages.

Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.

Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.

Only download apps from trusted sources like official app stores.

Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized transactions.



**For UPI safety:** Remember that receiving money does NOT require a PIN.

**For UPI safety:** Double-check the recipient name before sending money.
